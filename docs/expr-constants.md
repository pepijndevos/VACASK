# Builtin Constants

Builtin constants are predefined identifiers available in all expressions. They cannot be redefined. The names match the constants defined in the Verilog-A `constants.vams` header.

## Mathematical constants

| Constant | Value | Description |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| `M_E` | $e$ | Euler's number, $e = \exp(1)$ |
| `M_LOG2E` | $\log_2 e$ | Base-2 logarithm of $e$ |
| `M_LOG10E` | $\log_{10} e$ | Base-10 logarithm of $e$ |
| `M_LN2` | $\ln 2$ | Natural logarithm of 2 |
| `M_LN10` | $\ln 10$ | Natural logarithm of 10 |
| `M_PI` | $\pi$ | Pi |
| `M_TWO_PI` | $2\pi$ | Two times pi |
| `M_PI_2` | $\pi/2$ | Pi over 2 |
| `M_PI_4` | $\pi/4$ | Pi over 4 |
| `M_1_PI` | $1/\pi$ | Reciprocal of pi |
| `M_2_PI` | $2/\pi$ | Two over pi |
| `M_2_SQRTPI` | $2/\sqrt{\pi}$ | Two over square root of pi |
| `M_SQRT2` | $\sqrt{2}$ | Square root of 2 |
| `M_SQRT1_2` | $1/\sqrt{2}$ | Reciprocal of square root of 2 |
| `M_DEGPERRAD` | $180/\pi$ | Degrees per radian |
